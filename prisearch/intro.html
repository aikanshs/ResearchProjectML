PRISearch

In this model we predict whether a given pair of protein and RNA sequence interacts or not.

Protein and RNA interaction plays important roles in wide variety of biological processes. There are already some high throughputs experimetnal way to test whether a given pair interacts or not but those methods are expensive and at times are not feasible. So, we have developed a basic computational model which can easily predict protein-RNA interaction and is less time consuming.

PRISearch has been developed using machine learning classifier Support Vector Machine.SVMs are classifiers mainly used for classfication analysis.For prediction of the given pair we have trained our machine on a benchmark dataset PRI2241 using SVM.
